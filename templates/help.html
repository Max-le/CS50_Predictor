Help section

Are you a footbal enthousiast ?
This site shows upcoming football matches of your league of choice and lets you give a prediction on the outcome.
Give it your best shot and earn points for correct guesses !

  • In Home Page, you can see a list of the next fixtures for the selected league. You can place a guess by clicking the green button "Place bet"
  • In My Predictions, you can see all the guesses you've made. Once the match is finished "To be Defined" will be replaced by the actual score.

This is not a betting website, you won't be able to spend any money here. You make guesses for free and earn virtual points, that's it.
